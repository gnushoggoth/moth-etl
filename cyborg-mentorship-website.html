About CYBORG

CYBORG offers a unique service: companionship from retired male gamers who understand the value of respectful interaction. We provide women with judgment-free company from men who have left behind the competitive gaming scene but carry forward the skills of teamwork, strategy, and genuine conversation.

Our companions offer platonic friendship and engaging conversation without the pressures or expectations often found in typical social interactions. Whether you want to learn about gaming, have someone to accompany you to an event, or simply enjoy relaxed conversation over coffee, our CYBORG companions are here to provide respectful, enjoyable company.
